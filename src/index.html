Some of 0ur Ongoing Work
Liquefaction of biodegradable organic solid waste, to be used as feed to Anaerobic digester to produce biogas/ CBG.
Single stage automatic segregation of MSW in to biodegradable slurry & non- biodegradable material. The slurry to be used as direct feed to biogas plants. The non-biodegradable material be used as RDF, or sent to MRF for recovery of various fractions.
Developing dewatering press for separation of liquid from MSW to be used as feedstock for biogas generation, and the dry solid portion for conversion to compost.
